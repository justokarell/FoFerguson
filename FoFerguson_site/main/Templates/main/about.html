ABOUT

The Friends of The Ferguson Library operates the only used book shop in lower Fairfield County, and the only independent book shop in Stamford. The Shop has locations at the Main Library and Harry Bennett Branch and is open during Library hours. The Book Shop sells gently used books, movies and music at reasonable prices. All items are donated by the community, and most books are priced at $5 or less. All proceeds support the Library.
