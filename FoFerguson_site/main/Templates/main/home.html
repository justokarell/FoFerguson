The Friends Bookshop

The used bookshop of the Ferguson Library in Stamford, CT

ABOUT

We are the used bookstore of the Ferguson Library of Stamford, CT. As a result, we are, physically and financially, part of the library itself. All proceeds go to the staff and programs of the Ferguson Library.

INVENTORY

Search our inventory for books in our 'Main' branch, which is attached to the Ferguson Library in Downtown Stamford.
